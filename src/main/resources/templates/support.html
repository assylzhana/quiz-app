Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

▼
How do I start a quiz?
To start a quiz, simply click on the "Start Quiz" button on the homepage. You will be directed to a page where you can select the quiz you want to take.
▼
Can I retake a quiz?
Yes, you can retake a quiz. After completing a quiz, you will have the option to retake it if you wish to improve your score.
▼
How are quiz scores calculated?
Quiz scores are calculated based on the number of correct answers you provide. Each correct answer earns you points, and your total score is displayed at the end of the quiz.
▼
Is there a time limit for quizzes?
Yes, some quizzes may have a time limit. The time limit, if applicable, will be mentioned on the quiz instructions page. Make sure to complete the quiz within the specified time.
▼
How can I contact support?
If you have any questions or need assistance, you can contact our support team by visiting the "Support" page on our website. We'll be happy to help you.
▼
Are there different quiz categories available?
Yes, we offer a variety of quiz categories to choose from. You can explore different topics and subjects to find quizzes that interest you.
▼
Can I create my own quiz?
Currently, we do not offer the ability for users to create their own quizzes. However, we are continuously adding new quizzes to our platform.
▼
How do I reset my quiz progress?
To reset your quiz progress, you can go to your profile settings and find the option to reset quiz history. Please note that this action is irreversible.
▼
What happens if I lose my internet connection during a quiz?
If you lose your internet connection during a quiz, your progress will be automatically saved. You can resume the quiz from where you left off when you reconnect.
